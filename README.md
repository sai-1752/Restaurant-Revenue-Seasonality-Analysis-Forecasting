# 📊 Restaurant Revenue Seasonality Analysis & Forecasting

This project provides an in-depth analysis of a restaurant's multi-year revenue data, identifies recurring seasonal patterns, and proposes actionable strategies to optimize performance. It also demonstrates strong customer handling skills through a real-world complaint scenario.

---

## 🔍 Key Observations

- 📈 The venue experienced **consistent growth** from early 2023 to early 2025.
- 🎯 **Revenue peaked** in **December 2024**.
- 📉 A seasonal dip was noted in **April 2025**, aligning with historical trends.
- 🔮 **Forecasting** suggests a gradual recovery starting from **June 2025**.

---

## 📅 Seasonality Patterns

| Season           | Performance Level | Insight                                          |
|------------------|-------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Nov–Dec (Festive)| High              | Peak performance due to seasonal demand          |
| Mar–Apr          | Low               | Post-holiday drop in demand                      |
| May–Jul          | Moderate          | Stable, with room for improvement                |

---

## 💡 Actionable Insights

- 🛍️ Launch **limited-time offers** or **bundle deals** in April to counteract seasonal dips.
- 🎁 Introduce a **weekday loyalty program** to improve mid-week sales.
- 📲 Use **targeted digital promotions** during historically low-performing months.
- 📊 **Monitor campaign performance monthly** to adjust strategies based on real-time trends.

## 🔧 Problem Solving: Customer Complaint Handling

### 🗣️ Complaint:
> *"We are having too many issues with the system, please take your devices back and we would like to cancel the contract."*

### ✅ Professional Response:
> "I understand your frustration, and I sincerely apologize that our system hasn't been meeting your expectations.  
> Before discussing cancellation, I'd like to schedule a call within the next 24 hours to understand the issues fully.  
> Many technical problems can be resolved quickly once identified, and I want to ensure we've exhausted all options before a final decision."

### 📌 Follow-Up Actions:
1. 🚨 **Emergency escalation** to tech support and management.
2. 🔍 **Rapid assessment** via remote diagnosis or on-site visit within 48 hours.
3. 📝 **Document issues** in a detailed log to prevent future occurrences.
4. 🛠️ **Provide interim solutions** where needed.
5. 📆 **Set clear deadlines** and provide regular progress updates.
